Class that might be in a restaurant

Class: Entree

Attributes:
name (string)
pairings (array)
calories (integer)
cook_time (integer)
is_gluten_free (boolean)

Methods:
make_name_french (modifies name)
salad_not_frenchfies (adds 'salad' and removes 'french fries' from pairings array)
add_cheese (adds 100 to calories)
well_done (adds 5 to cook_time)
use_gluten_free_ingredients (sets is_gluten_free to true)
